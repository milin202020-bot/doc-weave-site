Frequently asked questions

Is this a subscription?

No. DocWeave licences are a one-time purchase and include all updates within the same major version (e.g. all 1.x releases). Major upgrades may be offered at an upgrade price.

Can I try before buying?

Yes — every download includes a free, fully-featured 30-day trial. No card required.

How is a licence delivered?

After purchase you'll receive a licence key by email. It activates DocWeave on your machine — open ⚙ → Licence & Account and paste it in.

What's your refund policy?

14-day money-back guarantee. If DocWeave doesn't work for you, email support@doc-weave.co.in within 14 days for a full refund.

Do you store my documents?

No. DocWeave is a local desktop app — your projects, screenshots, and exports stay on your machine. See the in-app Privacy Policy for details.
