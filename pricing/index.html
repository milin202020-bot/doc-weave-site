Frequently asked questions

Lifetime or Annual — which should I choose?

Choose Lifetime if you want to pay once and use DocWeave indefinitely (including all 1.x updates). Choose Annual if you want to stay on the latest major version as the product evolves — useful for teams who need the newest export formats and content types each year. Annual costs less upfront and you can cancel anytime.

How do I pay?

After you email us your Machine ID, we'll reply with a PayPal invoice. Once payment clears, we generate your licence key and email it — usually within a few hours on business days. If you prefer bank transfer or UPI (India), just mention it in your email and we'll arrange it.

Can I try before buying?

Yes — every download includes a free, fully-featured 15-day trial. No card required, no account needed. Just download, install, and start building.

How is a licence delivered?

After we receive your Machine ID, we generate a key cryptographically locked to your machine and email it to you. Open DocWeave → ⚙ → Licence & Account, paste the key and your email address, and click Activate.

What happens when my Annual subscription renews?

We'll email you an invoice before the renewal date. Once paid, we issue a new key valid for another year. If you choose not to renew, your current key stays active until its expiry date — your documents are always yours.

Can I use the licence on more than one PC?

Each key is tied to a single machine (Solo and Pro). If you change your PC or reinstall Windows, email us and we'll reissue the key for your new Machine ID at no charge. The Team plan covers multiple seats — include all Machine IDs when ordering.

What's your refund policy?

14-day money-back guarantee. If DocWeave doesn't work for you, email support@doc-weave.co.in within 14 days of purchase for a full refund.

Do you store my documents?

No. DocWeave is a local desktop app — your projects, screenshots, and exports stay on your machine. See the in-app Privacy Policy for details.
