Features & Release Notes

What's new in DocWeave

DocWeave turns annotated screenshots into polished SOPs, runbooks, and training guides — and it keeps getting better. Here's everything built in, and what each release adds.

Built in from day one

Everything you need to document a process end-to-end — in one Windows app, fully offline.

📷

Advanced screen capture

Region, window, full-screen, or long-page scroll capture, with a global hotkey and an automatic SOP recorder.

✏️

Professional annotation

Numbered callouts, arrows, highlight boxes, blur & redaction, and spotlight — non-destructive, originals always kept.

🧩

20+ content block types

Quizzes, checklists, data tables, flowcharts, timelines, RACI matrices, video embeds, warnings, and more.

📤

Multi-format export

One document → PDF, HTML, Word, PowerPoint, Markdown, or ZIP. Every export is self-contained.

🎨

Custom branding

Your logo, accent colours, and themes on every export. Export profiles save one-click settings per client.

🔒

Local & private

Runs entirely on your PC. No cloud account, no data collection — your screenshots never leave your machine.

Release history

Recent updates, newest first.

v1.1.5 Latest 27 June 2026
Rich text everywhere & smarter layout
  • Markdown in every export — bold, italic, and bullet lists in step descriptions now render in PDF, Word, PowerPoint, and Present mode — not just HTML.
  • Guided start — new projects open with a ready-made starter outline, so you're never staring at a blank canvas.
  • Callouts & long text fit cleanly — step callouts no longer cover the highlighted area, and long descriptions auto-fit instead of being cut off in PDF and PowerPoint.
  • Reliable drag-and-drop — reordering screens now drops them exactly where you aim.
v1.1.4 26 June 2026
Safer editing — undo/redo that actually works
  • Undo/redo in text boxes — type in any step box, caption, or table cell and Ctrl+Z undoes it, Ctrl+Y redoes.
  • Undo no longer loses work — undoing a delete or move used to wipe edits made afterward and bring deleted screens back. Every edit is now tracked, so undo steps back one action at a time.
  • Drag multiple screens at once — select several items and drag to reorder them together, in one undoable move.
v1.1.3 25 June 2026
Consistent step numbering across every export
  • Same step numbers everywhere — PDF, HTML, Word, PowerPoint, Markdown, and Present mode now show identical step numbers.
  • Restart numbering from any step — begin a fresh 1, 2, 3… sequence wherever you like.
  • Live export number — the editor shows the exact number a step will have in your exports.
v1.1.2 29 May 2026
Production hardening — security, stability & trust
  • Security — updated dependencies to clear known advisories; added a code-signing step for signed builds.
  • Stability — corrupt or password-protected imports now show a friendly error instead of crashing.
  • Legal & accessibility — added EULA, Privacy Policy and Terms; screen readers now announce toolbar buttons.
v1.1.0 28 May 2026
User-experience polish & navigation overhaul
  • Content-type gallery — a visual picker for all 25+ content types when adding an item.
  • Pin & navigate — pin items to the top, sticky section headers, and a Windows taskbar jumplist for recent projects.
  • Project Manager — thumbnails, archiving, and richer recent-project handling.
  • Export options — HTML light/dark themes, review comments in exports, and filename-collision handling.
